About Me:

I’m a contemporary visual artist specializing in mixed media, abstract painting, and photography. My work explores themes of identity, memory, and the intersection of digital and physical worlds. With over 5 years of experience, my art has been displayed in galleries across the country and featured in several international art fairs. My goal is to provoke thought and engage the viewer with a sense of wonder through the fusion of traditional and modern techniques.
